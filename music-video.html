Animated Music Video

One of my coursework units for my Cambridge Technical in Digital Media was to create a music video to a song of my choice. I chose the EDM track Hydrogen by M.O.O.N.. The track is known for its feature in the soundtrack of the 2012 video game Hotline Miami, which heavily inspired the narrative of the video.

The video itself was created within the video game Garry's Mod, where I used animation and camera tools to manipulate 3D model rigs alongside animations found in the game. I was not originally going to create the video in this way, but pandemic lockdowns in early 2021 prohibited me from accessing school grounds and gathering enough people to film in person. I then edited the footage using Adobe Premiere Pro.

As part of my coursework requirements, I had to create a number of pre-production and research documents to not only plan the creation of the video but also show how it would appeal to the target audience and conform to the conventions of existing music videos. All of my Digital Media coursework is available on my blogger page at dkerrymedia.blogspot.com. My music video project is Unit 3.
